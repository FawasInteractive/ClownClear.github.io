About the Game

Clown Clear is an intense FPS action horror featuring grisly combat against the clown-like hosts of a diabolically-destructive zombie virus.

Play as one of six different characters and face off against ravenous hordes of the infected in this unique spin on the critically acclaimed zombie genre. Put your skills to the test as you run, stab, shoot, and blast your way through the apocalypse using a plethora of devastating weapons and items.

Gameplay

Engage in thrilling fast-paced action as you fight against relentless hordes of the infected in three separate game-modes: Campaign, Survival, or Build.

Progress through a character dependent story, scavenge for resources, and strategically plan your next move in surviving the killer clown onslaught.

Experience the adrenaline rush of surviving against a selection of especially mutated special clowns, who use their superior intelligence and abilities to throw you off course.
