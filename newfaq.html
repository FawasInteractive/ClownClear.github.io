Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What type of game is Clown Clear?

Clown Clear is a first-person shooter game with a heavy focus on replayability. Maps change, special enemy spawns are dynamic, and campaigns are designed to be fun to play multiple times.

Who is developing / publishing Clown Clear?

Fawas Interactive is developing and publishing Clown Clear.

When does Clown Clear release?

Clown Clear’s release date is currently to be determined, but it’s on track for October 2026.

Which platform(s) will Clown Clear release on?

Clown Clear will release on Steam; other platforms have yet to be determined.

How much will Clown Clear cost?

The price is currently to be determined, but somewhere between $9.99 to $19.99 can be expected.

What are the system requirements to run Clown Clear?

Clown Clear's minimal system requirements are to be determined.

What's the history of Clown Clear?

The game originally started as a simple wave-based shooter with a single weapon and map before evolving into what it is today. It's been in active development since 2019 and is estimated to release around Fall 2026.

Content Questions

What is Clown Clear's setting?

Clown Clear is set in the an apocalyptic Southern United States. Set closely near Halloween, the game will lean into its eerie autumn aesthetic.

Who are Clown Clear's characters?

Noah, Jade, Sarge, Eddy, Gregory, and Charlotte are immune individuals trying to survive the infection, also serving as the protagonists and playable characters.

What enemies will Clown Clear feature?

Players will need to watch out for a variety of especially mutated clowns that fight alongside the horde, each with their own special abilities.

What is the story in Clown Clear’s gameplay?

Each storyline is made of of playable campaigns, following a specific survivor as they try to accomplish a set of goals.

What game modes will Clown Clear feature?

Clown Clear is planned to have multiple game modes, such as an infinite "Survival" mode, along with the standard "Campaign" mode.

Can I play Clown Clear co-operatively?

Simple multiplayer support is being tested for Clown Clear, but there are no guarantees.

Will Clown Clear have a progression system?

Besides progressing through the story (with players able to skip campaigns, or ignore the story entirely), Clown Clear will not release with an overarching progression system. A similar system is in the works, but it’ll likely be in a post-release update.

Will there be microtransactions or DLC content?

There are currently no plans for microtransactions like purchase-able coins, but DLC content is very likely.

What game engine is Clown Clear being built on?

Clown Clear is being built on S&box (by Facepunch).

Will Clown Clear contain modding support?

Mod support is a high priority, and will most definitely come in some form. The extent to which the players can affect content is yet to be determined though due to time constraints.
